Theme

The website theme is modern, professional, and approachable, with a personal touch that reflects your aspirations. It balances professionalism with creativity, making it
suitable for showcasing your skills, personality, and career goals.

  1. Color Scheme: The deep purple tones (#4b0082 and #6a0dad) used for the header, footer, and hover effects give the site a sophisticated and professional look,
    while also adding a creative and energetic vibe. The background is light (#f9f9f9), which enhances readability and contrasts well with the purple accents.
  2. Rounded Elements: The use of rounded borders on images (border-radius: 10px;) softens the design and adds a friendly, approachable touch to the otherwise professional look.
  3. User-Friendly Navigation: The navigation menu is centered and easy to read, with bold, white text on a purple background. The hover effects add a dynamic element,
    making the site feel interactive and engaging.
  4. Minimalist Layout: The overall layout is clean and simple, with content centered on the page. This minimalist design ensures that the focus remains on the content (like the images and text), without unnecessary distractions.
